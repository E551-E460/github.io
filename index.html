What is BMI ?

The BMI range is 18.5 to 24.9. A healthy weight Maintaining a healthy weight may reduce your chances of developing health problems later in life, such as obesity and type 2 diabetes. Aim for a nutritious diet that is low in fat and sugar and includes plenty of fruits and vegetables. In addition, get regular physical activity, ideally about 30 minutes a day, five days a week.

What are the different BMI categories?

Under BMI classification categories are: Less than 18.5, Very thin from 18.5 to 24.9, Normal weight from 25 to 29.9, Overweight from 30 and above.

Are there any general tips to improve health?

Make sure to drink plenty of water, try to maintain your physical activity regularly, and avoid processed foods and excess sugars.

How can I have a proper diet?

Based on the BMI classification, we can determine the appropriate type of diet. If your weight is normal, your goal is to maintain your weight by following a balanced diet and exercising. If you are overweight or obese, you may need to reduce calories and improve your meal plan, while consulting a nutritionist. If you are thin, you may need to increase calories and eat foods rich in protein.
